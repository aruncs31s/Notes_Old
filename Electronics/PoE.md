# PoE

## Prerequisites

[[Ethernet]]

#### 802.3af/at (Standard PoE):

1. Power on data pairs (Mode A):

- Pin 1: Positive DC
- Pin 2: Positive DC
- Pin 3: Negative DC
- Pin 6: Negative DC

2. Power on spare pairs (Mode B):

- Pin 4: Positive DC
- Pin 5: Positive DC
- Pin 7: Negative DC
- Pin 8: Negative DC
