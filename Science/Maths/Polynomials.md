### Roots of Polynomial

#### Quadratic
#example 
$$
x^2 + x - 16 = 0
$$
#solution 

$$
x = \frac{-b \underset {-}{+}\sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

#### Cubic
#example 
$$
x^3 + px +q = 0
$$
#solution 

$$


x = \sqrt[3]{\frac{-b + \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}} + \sqrt[3]{\frac{-b - \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}}
$$
