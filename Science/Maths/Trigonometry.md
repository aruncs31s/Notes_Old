### Identities
1. Unit Hypotenuse
$$
\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1
$$
2. sico + cosi
$$
\sin(x+y) = \sin x \cos y + \cos x \sin y 
$$
3. coco - sisi
$$
   \cos\left(x+y\right) = \cos x \cos y - \sin x \sin y
$$

### Properties
1. $\sin(90-x) = \cos(x)$
1. $\cos(90-x) = \sin(x)$
